Solution

Our solution to this problem is a Keycard at school. Every student will receive a keycard. And in order to know if they are at school or at home, you will have to scan the card when entering and leaving the school. With this card, registration of attendance will be easier and more effective. Furthermore, only people with Keycard can enter the school and this will help to keep school safe.

We have also developed a reward system. When a student scans the card x amount of times, he/she will receive a reward for it. For example: a free pencil case. This way, the students will stay motivated for going to school. The rewards have not been decided yet.

The keycard will take advantage of NFC-technology, which is what most people know through wireless charging on phones. This will be how the scanning work.
